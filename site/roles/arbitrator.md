# Arbitrator

> *Stand between the blade and the throat. Pay the price if you must.*

You are the Loyalist bodyguard. Each night, you stand between the Murderer and their target. If the target is struck, *you* take the killing blow instead and they survive.

This is your power and your cost: protection by martyrdom.

**Night action — Guard.** One target. If they are hit, you die in their place.

**Drift cost:** +1 per night you guard.

**Stacking with the Chirurgeon:** if both you and a Chirurgeon protect the same target, the protection *stacks*. Two defenders, two absorptions — useful for high-traffic nights when the Murderer is moving.

**Self-targeting:** you may guard yourself. Useful on nights when you are publicly named as a Heretic.

**Play this role if:** you want to absorb aggression and force the Murderer to overextend.

**Loyalists should look for:** the player who has *never* been killed despite being a target. They may be the Arbitrator. Also look for the player who was *killed yesterday* while sitting next to a publicly named suspect — that is the Arbitrator's epitaph.

**Heretics should look for:** if you cannot land a kill on a target with two defenders, switch the kill to a third defender. Strip the cell of its shields.

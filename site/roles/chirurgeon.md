# Chirurgeon

> *Cut what needs cutting. Close what needs closing.*

You are the Loyalist healer. Each night, you designate one operative to protect. If they are struck by a Murderer, a Trap, or an assassination chain, *you* absorb the hit and they survive.

**Night action — Protect.** One target. They survive the night's strikes.

**Drift cost:** +1 per night you protect.

**Coverage rules:**
- If both you *and* the Arbitrator protect the same target on the same night, the protection *stacks* (two defenders, two strikes absorbed). Do this when you know the Murderer is moving.
- Your protection does not save a Tier 3 Interrogation victim if *they* are the target — Interrogation is a day action, not a night strike.

**Play this role if:** you want to be the cell's insurance policy.

**Loyalists should look for:** a Chirurgeon who protects the same person every night is *wasting* drift. They should rotate: protect the Interrogator one night, the Priest the next, the Citizen the next.

**Heretics should look for:** if a player is *never* dying despite being publicly named — they are defended. The defender is a T1 role. Find who is also at low drift and never seen acting, and you may have your Chirurgeon.
